# Introduction to Champions

Published on: 2025-03-21
Welcome back, Commanders!

Today, we're diving into how to get and strengthen your champions in DC: Dark Legion. Let's build your dream team!

## Obtaining Champions

1. Pre-Registration Rewards:

- Harley Quinn (Recommended): Best early boost, synergizes with 7-day login.
- Batman (Good): Solid choice, especially for light spenders.
- Green Lantern (Heavy Spenders Only): Powerful, but paywalled.
- Skip Wonder Woman and Flash (easily obtainable in-game).

2. 7-Day Login Rewards:

- Get 10 free Harley Quinn shards!

3. Path of Justice (Complete quests for a Mythic Hero Selector.):

- Poison Ivy (Recommended): Great healer and damage dealer.
- Lex Luthor (Legion of Doom Focus): Solid choice.

4. Purchasing Shards:

- Batman Starter Pack: Good value.
- Daily Deals: Purchase small amounts of shards for different heros, like Lex Luthor or Poison Ivy.

5. Mother Boxes:

- Decode for shards and other rewards. Aim for Divine Energy Keys (red boxes) for Mythic shards.

6. The Bleed:

- Use World Anvil Fragments to pull Mythic and Legendary champions.
- Focus on Interstellar Visitor (weekends) and Supreme Commander to get fragments.
- Plan ahead, and save fragments for the bleed characters that you really want.

7. Custom Mythic Champion Shards:

- Earn through Darkest Timeline and seasonal events.
- Select specific Mythic shards (excluding bleed characters).

8. Stores:

- Mystery Store: World Anvil fragments (prioritize 60 and 180 gem options).
- League Store: Shazam or Flash (use sparingly; focus on Quantum Components).
- Metabrawl store: Bane and Raven shards.

## Powering Up Champions:

1. Levels:

- Increase War Room level to raise champion level cap.
- Focus on your top 5 champions.

2. Stars:

- Obtain duplicates through The Bleed and Mother Boxes.
- Legendary characters are easier to star up.

3. Legacy Pieces:

- Obtain through The Bleed, Monitor's Legacy Pass, and House of Secrets.
- Match stats to champion roles (e.g., attack for damage dealers, defense for tanks).

4. Force (ACDC Shards):

- Upgrades champion abilities.
- Focus on percentage-based upgrades.

5. Gear:

- Craft and upgrade gear in the Armory.
- Deconstruct excess gear for building blocks.
- Look for gear with tag bonuses that match your team.

Note on Champion Reset:

- Reset champions level 1-300 (excluding Force).
- Only level 5 champions to save resources.

## Key Takeaways

- New Players should prioritize Harley Quinn and Poison Ivy for any free champion selectors or events.
- Save your gems for Interstellar Visitor so you can buy anvils to be used in the Bleed.
